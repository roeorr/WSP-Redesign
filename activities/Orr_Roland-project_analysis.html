Site Intent

The purpose of the redesign is to increase the conversion rate of users by giving the website a great sense of usability. We will need to clean up the layout as it is very confusing to any user trying to access the site. We will apply proper html semantics to correct the current markup for Top Stone, Inc. website.

The website is structured very poorly so we will need to make the following changes to the website to create a better conversion rate:

  • Create a six page website in which I am going to place the logo in the header and the navigation at the top of all pages.
  • Make the CTA the websites catalog so users can see what the company has to offer
  • Give the user a brief summary on who Top Stone. Inc. is and why they should purchase goods from Top Stone, Inc.
  • Organize the content structure so users can navigate through the website efficiently to increase the overall conversion rate.

Targeted Audience

We are going to target Caucasian women and men between the ages of 40-65. We will focus on those men and women who have a college education and no children between the targeted age. We wil focus on women in residential communities who make $60-$100k. We will focus on women and men who make $250-$750k with the commercial approach to the redesign
